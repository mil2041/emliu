About

I am currently a postdoctoral associate at Weill Cornell Medicine. I graduated from Weill Cornell Medicine with a PhD in Physiology, Biophysics and Systems Biology in 2019 under the supervision of Dr. Ekta Khurana. My research focuses on applying statistcal analysis and machine learning to large-scale multi-omics data in human cancers.
